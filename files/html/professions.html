Summer Jobs:

During the summers of 2021, 2022, and 2023, I had the opportunity to work at Rovanapa OY, where I was responsible for property maintenance for KAS Asunnot.


In this role, I gained valuable experience in ensuring the upkeep and functionality of residential properties.


From conducting repairs and maintenance tasks to addressing tenant concerns, I developed essential problem-solving and multitasking skills.


These summer jobs allowed me to contribute to the smooth operation of the properties and provided me with hands-on experience in a professional setting.

On-The-Job Learning:

In both my 8th and 9th grades, I participated in the On-The-Job Learning (OTJ) program, which provided me with practical work experience and insights into various industries.


During my TET placement at Power Rovaniemi, I gained exposure to the retail sector and assisted with tasks such as stocking shelves, organizing merchandise, and providing customer service.


This experience helped me develop customer interaction skills and an understanding of the importance of teamwork and effective communication in a retail environment.


Additionally, I had a TET placement at DAHL, where I contributed to warehouse cleaning activities.


This role required attention to detail and the ability to work efficiently to maintain a clean and organized warehouse.


Through this experience, I learned the importance of maintaining a safe and orderly work environment, as well as the value of teamwork and accountability in a warehouse setting.


While my earlier work experiences may not directly relate to my chosen field of study in vocational school, they have provided me with transferable skills such as responsibility, time management, teamwork, and adaptability.


These experiences have shaped my work ethic and given me a solid foundation to build upon as I enter the industry.

Summary:

Although I am yet to embark on a professional career within my chosen field, my earlier work experiences have allowed me to develop important skills and work principles.


I am eager to apply the knowledge gained from vocational school, coupled with my previous work experiences, to pursue a successful and fulfilling career.


I am confident that my strong work ethic, dedication, and continuous commitment to learning will enable me to make a positive impact in the industry.
