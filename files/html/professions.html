Summer Jobs:

2021, 2022 & 2023:

During 3 weeks of the summer holidays in the years 2021, 2022 and 2023, I worked at KAS Asunnot OY as a member of the team for the maintenance of the real estate.

Usually it was just mowing the grass around the properties and picking up the rubbish that people had left on the ground around the property.

These summer jobs allowed me to contribute to the smooth running of the properties and and gave me practical experience in a professional environment.

On-The-Job learning:

2021:

In 2021, I was working at Power, an electronics store, where I put various items on shelves, installed alarm systems on the expensive products, unpacked items that came in boxes to the warehouse and later put them in their slots on the shelves.

2020:

In 2020, I worked at Dahl for 1 week. Dahl is the leading wholesaler of HVAC, municipal and industrial piping products in the Nordic and Baltic Sea region. My job was to clean the warehouses, put the products in their proper places on the shelves and unpack the new products that came into the warehouse through the loading bay.

2019:

In 2019, I spent a day working in the canteen of my school, where I was at the time. I did a variety of tasks, including cleaning tables, washing dishes, and taking food in and out.
